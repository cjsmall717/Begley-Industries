Notes

Inspirations

Beagley Industries was inspired by a long-lived, small companion. The surname "Small" is an Anglicised form of the Irish O' Beaglaoich. Although my dog is not a beagle, her likeness was used in an early beer label as a family joke. The idea proved too good to discard, and became the basis for the name and logo.
